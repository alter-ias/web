1. Executive Summary

Alter.Lab is pleased to present this proposal for the creation of 5-6 minutes of graphic animation for the DREAM250 project. We understand the significance of this project as a historical and cultural commemoration. Our approach focuses on blending the efficiency of generative AI with the sensibility of concept art and handcrafted animation to produce a visually stunning, emotive, and cohesive piece that honors the project's narrative. Both options presented below guarantee an exceptional quality outcome, offering different levels of customization and creative depth.

2. Project Understanding

We have analyzed the structure of the piece, divided into 5 key segments (Prologue, Becoming American, The Revolutionary Promise, The Promise in Peril, The Promise Pending). We understand the goal is to create a visual tapestry that weaves together Lavinia Meijer's performance, dance, keywords, historical quotes, and an aesthetic that evolves from cosmic to contemporary, using a mix of generative art and archival footage.

Our hybrid methodology allows us to create complex and fluid visuals while respecting the historical integrity and emotional tone of each segment.

3. Work Proposals

Below, we present two options designed to offer distinct levels of collaboration and production.

OPTION 1: ESSENTIAL CREATIVE PACKAGE

  • Concept & Art Direction: Development of one (1) primary visual style based on the creative brief, with key hand-drawn concept art for pivotal moments.
  • Animation Technique: A strong emphasis on Generative AI for the bulk of the visual sequences, supported by 2D animation for text and transitions.
  • Revision Process: Includes two (2) rounds of revisions for each of the 3 main phases (Storyboard, Animation Draft, Final Cut).
  • Deliverables: Final 5-6 minute piece in 4K resolution, master file, and optimized versions for streaming and projection.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $37,500 USD

OPTION 2: PREMIUM PRODUCTION PACKAGE

  • Concept & Art Direction (Extended): Exploration and presentation of up to three (3) distinct creative directions and a comprehensive concept art dossier.
  • Animation Technique (Advanced Hybrid): A balance of AI with significant integration of traditional hand-drawn 2D animation for high-impact emotional moments.
  • Revision Process (Flexible): Includes three (3) rounds of revisions per phase, plus a dedicated post-production and color grading session.
  • Deliverables: Everything from Option 1, plus a promotional package of 10 high-resolution stills and 10 short video clips.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $43,500 USD
