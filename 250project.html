Part I: Graphic Animation Proposal

2. Project Understanding

We have analyzed the structure of the animation, divided into 5 key segments (Prologue, Becoming American, The Revolutionary Promise, The Promise in Peril, The Promise Pending). We understand the goal is to create a visual tapestry that weaves together Lavinia Meijer's performance, dance, keywords, and historical quotes, using a mix of generative art and archival footage.

3. Work Proposals

Below, we present two options for the animation production.

OPTION 1: ESSENTIAL CREATIVE PACKAGE

  • Concept & Art Direction: Development of one (1) primary visual style based on the creative brief, with key hand-drawn concept art for pivotal moments.
  • Animation Technique: A strong emphasis on Generative AI for the bulk of the visual sequences, supported by 2D animation for text and transitions.
  • Revision Process: Includes two (2) rounds of revisions for each of the 3 main phases (Storyboard, Animation Draft, Final Cut).
  • Deliverables: Final 5-6 minute piece in 4K resolution, master file, and optimized versions.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $37,500 USD

OPTION 2: PREMIUM PRODUCTION PACKAGE

  • Concept & Art Direction (Extended): Exploration and presentation of up to three (3) distinct creative directions and a comprehensive concept art dossier.
  • Animation Technique (Advanced Hybrid): A balance of AI with significant integration of traditional hand-drawn 2D animation for high-impact emotional moments.
  • Revision Process (Flexible): Includes three (3) rounds of revisions per phase, plus a dedicated post-production and color grading session.
  • Deliverables: Everything from Option 1, plus a promotional package of 10 high-resolution stills and 10 short video clips.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $43,500 USD

Part II: Web Development Proposal

6. Philosophy & Technology

Our approach is grounded in 100% custom development, without the use of templates, to deliver a superior final product.

  • GitHub Repository: All source code will be managed via a private GitHub repository, ensuring professional version control and transparent collaboration.
  • Cutting-Edge Architecture (Jamstack): We build pre-rendered static sites for exceptional loading speed, robust security, and seamless scalability to handle any traffic spikes.
  • Flexible Content Management (Headless CMS): We integrate a Headless CMS (like Sanity.io or Strapi), providing your team with an intuitive dashboard to update all content in real-time without needing a developer.

7. Work Proposals

OPTION 1: STRATEGIC ACCELERATED LAUNCH

Objective: To launch a high-quality, 100% custom website on an urgent timeline, establishing a strong technical foundation.

  • Features: Custom UI/UX Design, Front-End development from scratch (React/Next.js or similar), one-page design with up to 5 sections, Headless CMS integration, subtle UI animations, and technical SEO.
  • Urgent Delivery Timeline: 3-4 weeks.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $8,500 USD

OPTION 2: FULL NARRATIVE PLATFORM

Objective: To evolve the initial site into a comprehensive multi-page platform that tells the full story of DREAM250.

  • Includes everything from Option 1, plus: Expansion to a multi-page site with custom templates (e.g., Press, Events), advanced GSAP animations, interactive components (e.g., event map), and an extended content model in the CMS.
  • Delivery Timeline: 6-8 additional weeks post-Phase 1.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $17,000 USD

OPTION 3: IMMERSIVE 3D EXPERIENCE

Objective: To position DREAM250 at the forefront of digital storytelling with an interactive 3D web experience.

  • Includes everything from Option 2, plus: A real-time 3D scene (WebGL/Three.js) integrated into the site, 3D art direction, modeling of 3D assets, and programming of user interaction logic.
  • Delivery Timeline: 12-16+ additional weeks post-Phase 1.

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $35,000+ USD*

*Final price depends on the complexity and number of 3D assets to be developed.

A Strategic Note on Investment: These investment levels have been adjusted from our standard rates for custom development. We are doing this to reflect our enthusiasm for the DREAM250 vision and our strong interest in building a long-term, integrated creative partnership with Karz Productions.
